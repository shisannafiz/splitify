Splitify is a way to organize music by adding songs from one playlist to many playlists based on the feeling a song gives

1

Select

Select the main playlist with the music to split up

2

Create or Choose

Create new playlists or choose old playlists to split into

3

Confirm

Confirm the selected and created/chosen playlists

4

Click

Click on a playlist to add the current playing song to it

5

Review

Review the updated playlists and remove tracks
